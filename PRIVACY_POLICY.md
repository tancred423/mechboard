# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 16 January 2026

## Data We Collect

### Account Data
When you log in with Discord, we receive and store:
- Discord user ID
- Username
- Avatar URL

### User Content
- Encounter configurations you create
- Folder organization preferences

## How We Use Data

- To provide and operate the service
- To authenticate your account
- To sync your encounters across devices

## Data Storage

- Account data and encounters are stored in our database
- Local encounters are stored in your browser's local storage
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account exists
- You can delete your encounters at any time

## Third-Party Services

- **Discord**: Used for authentication. Subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy)

## Your Rights

You can:
- Export your encounter data using the export feature
- Delete your encounters through the application
- Request account deletion by contacting us at mechboard@tancred.de

## Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, open an issue on the GitHub repository.
